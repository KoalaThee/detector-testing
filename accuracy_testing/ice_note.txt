pyhton yolo_track_img_reid.py --yolo-weights <yolov8n.pt.> --tracking-method <ocsort> --reid-model <osnet_x0_25_msmt17.pt> --source <path-to-folder-of-imgs> --output <path-to-results-inventory>
 
# yolo_track_img.py

python yolo_track_img.py --model <yolov8n.pt >--source <mot_eval/datasets/dataset1> --tracker <byetrack.yaml|botsort.yaml> --output tracking_results/img/d1_y8n_<bt|bs>.txt 


# rcnn_track_img.py

python boxmot_track_img.py --tracking-method bytetrack --reid-model <osnet_x0_25_msmt17.pt> --source <mot_eval/datasets/dataset1> --output <tracking_results/img/d1_y8n_bt_bm.txt>

Object Detector:
Ulterlytics: Yolo
Boxmot: Faster R CNN

Tracker:
Boxmot:
> OcSort (ocs) – motion-only, very fast, simple to integrate.
> ByteTrack (bt) – excellent accuracy/speed tradeoff, very popular.
> BotSort (bs) – ByteTrack + appearance + camera motion compensation.
> DeepOcSort (docs) – OC-SORT + appearance features (ReID).
> StrongSort (ss) – upgraded DeepSORT with better ReID (OSNet) and smoother motion model.
> BoostTrack (bst) – very strong accuracy but heavier.
> HybridSort (hbs)
